About Sifu Lloyd Wright

Sifu Lloyd Wright is a 4th Dan Black Belt in the Yee’s Hung Ga system of traditional Chinese martial arts. With over 25 years of teaching and a lifelong dedication to the art, he leads the Midtown NYC branch of the Hung Family Kung Fu school with humility, precision, and strength.

His lineage connects directly back to Grandmaster Frank Yee (Yee Chi Wai), and his classes emphasize not just combat techniques, but also discipline, health, and cultural tradition.

Students of all levels are welcome — from absolute beginners to seasoned martial artists looking to deepen their practice.
